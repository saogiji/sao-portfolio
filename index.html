A little more about me

Behind every brilliant AI is a human tirelessly teaching it the difference between a street sign and a tree, or stopping it from confidently making up fake facts. Hi, I’m Simon, and I am that human. As an Independent AI Trainer and Data Annotator, I build the pristine "Ground Truth" datasets that make Artificial Intelligence actually intelligent.

I didn't just stumble into this. I hold a bachelor's degree in Computer and Communications Engineering from Abubakar Tafawa Balewa University (2020) and completed an M.Sc. in Cyber Security & Digital Forensics in 2024. Driven by a deep obsession with data integrity and systems logic, I am also continuously expanding my enterprise AI skills through programs like Google GEAR.

Because of this technical rigor, I don't just label data; I build the automated analytical pipelines around it. I love combining custom API integrations, Google Apps Script, and advanced Excel modeling to transform chaotic inputs into highly organized workflows. I regularly apply this "wargaming" strategy to build automated prediction models for complex sports analytics (NBA, tennis, darts).

Beyond software, I apply this systems-thinking to the physical world. I am currently managing the establishment of an integrated palm oil plantation, successfully executing Phase 1 (24-hectare clearing and intercropping) toward a 200-hectare milestone. From planning high-efficiency hybrid power solutions (N-type solar and lithium battery storage) to scaling agile frameworks across organizations, my goal is to engineer workflows that scale.

If you’re thinking about hiring me for your next product, machine learning, or systems integration project, stop thinking and let's talk. My thirst for knowledge, technical rigor, and knack for excellence will make me an immediate asset to your team.
