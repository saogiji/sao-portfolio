A little more about me

Behind every brilliant AI is a human tirelessly teaching it the difference between a street sign and a tree, or stopping it from confidently making up fake facts. Hi, I’m Simon, and I am that human.

As an Independent AI Trainer, Data Annotator, and Analyst, my day job is literally teaching machines how to understand the real world. Whether I’m drawing complex bounding boxes on computer vision images, extracting text from messy documents via OCR, or engineering multi-turn prompts to keep Large Language Models (LLMs) from hallucinating, I build the pristine "Ground Truth" datasets that make Artificial Intelligence actually intelligent.

I didn't just stumble into this. I received my bachelor's degree in Computer and Communications Engineering from Abubakar Tafawa Balewa University in 2020. Curiosity drove me to complete a responsive web design course with freeCodeCamp in June 2022, where I learned how to create this simple responsive webpage. I completed EntryLevel's Product Management and Scrum Master courses in July and November 2022, respectively. Driven by a deep obsession with data integrity and systems logic, I went on to complete my M.Sc. in Cyber Security & Digital Forensics in 2024.

Because of this technical rigor, I don't just label data; I build the automated analytical pipelines around it. I love combining custom API integrations, JavaScript, and advanced Excel modeling to transform raw, chaotic inputs into highly organized, automated workflows. Ultimately, my goal is to engineer the evidence-based training datasets that significantly improve how AI systems operate, assisting organizations in building safe, reliable tech that makes people’s lives easier and stress-free.

If you’ve read this far, I'd love to learn something from you. And if you're thinking about hiring me for your next machine learning project, stop thinking and hire me; my thirst for knowledge, technical rigor, and knack for excellence will make me an immediate asset to your organization.
